Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is a fictional character in the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling. She is one of three main characters in the series working side by side with Harry Potter and Ron Weasley. Hermione is the brains of the trio, and one of the smartest people at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Hermione is known for her insecurity and is very fearful of failure.

Hermione is part of the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling.
