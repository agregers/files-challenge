Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is a fictional character in the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling. She is one of three main characters in the series working side by side with Harry Potter. Hermione is the brains of the trio, and one of the smartest people at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Later in the series, Hermione ends up dating Ron Weasely.
